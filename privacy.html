Privacy

PaprFeed shows public research metadata from arXiv, bioRxiv, medRxiv, and PubMed. Searches are sent through the app's Cloudflare proxy so the feed can load reliably.

Saved Papers

If you are not signed in, saved papers stay only in this browser. If you sign in with Google, saved papers are synced to Supabase so they can appear on your other devices.

Login

Google login is handled by Supabase and Google. PaprFeed does not receive or store your Google password.

Deleting Data

You can unsave papers by tapping Saved again when the paper appears in the feed or details view. If you want all cloud-synced saved-paper data deleted, contact the app owner from the place where this app was shared.
