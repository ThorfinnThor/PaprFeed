About

A calmer way to keep up with research

PaprFeed is a free research discovery app for browsing recent scientific papers without the noise of a traditional social feed. It brings public paper metadata from PubMed, arXiv, bioRxiv, and medRxiv into one searchable reading queue.

Built for focused discovery

Readers can search a topic, choose a research field, filter paper types, change the date range, and save useful papers. PaprFeed links back to the original record or publisher so readers can review the authoritative source.

Recent papers, clearly presented

Publication dates, journals, authors, abstracts, and identifiers are shown when the source provides them. Preprints are labeled separately from published articles, and source-specific metadata remains attributable to the service that supplied it.
