Why follow BCMA separately?

BCMA CAR-T has a fast-moving clinical literature with frequent updates on myeloma outcomes, resistance, toxicity, and post-relapse sequencing.

What should I watch for?

Look for durability, MRD negativity, antigen loss, immune reconstitution, neurotoxicity, and outcomes after prior bispecific or antibody exposure.
